Outdoors

  1. Home
  2. Outdoors

The house has a lovely shaded portico off the living and dining rooms with views of the garden and valley. There is an outside table under the portico and a separate seating area for tea or coffee or just relaxing.

Beyond the portico on the large tiled terrace is another table under umbrellas with seating for 8-9. This is next to a hedged ornamental garden with roses and lavender. During the summer most people prefer to eat outside.

Beyond the ornamental garden is a grassy area with a path leading down to steps that descend to the pool, beyond which there are more steps to a hillside olive grove. Everywhere has lovely views across farmland to Loro Piceno or other nearby towns. The side of the house has more views and a barbeque.

  • Shaded portico with eating area and casual seating
  • Large tiled terrace with eating area under umbrellas
  • Rose and lavender garden
  • Steps to pool area
  • Tiled pool area with sunbeds for nine people. Umbrellas and olive trees for shade
  • 10x4 metre pool. Shallow end suitable for children.
  • Steps to olive grove
  • BBQ at side of house
